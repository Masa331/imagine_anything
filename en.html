Who are we

We are software development company which specializes in web applications. The most experiences we have are with Linux, SQL, Ruby, HTTP, HTML & CSS and JavaScript. We know how to design functional user interfaces. We also know how to do customer support and how to further develop projects based on customer feedback.

Historically custom software development was always biggest part of our revenue. Now we are trying to transform into company with most of it's profits coming from running own projects. This is our main goal for 2020.

Our vision is to become a company involved with wider society through activities across various areas both in for-profit and non-profit sectors.
